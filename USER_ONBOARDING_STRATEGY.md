# Pilot User Onboarding Strategy
## Voice-First Public Accountability Platform - Shivpuri District

## Overview

A comprehensive onboarding strategy designed for common citizens in Shivpuri, Madhya Pradesh, with low to medium smartphone literacy and Hindi as primary language. The strategy focuses on trust-building, clear expectations, and voice-first interaction.

## Target User Profile

- **Location**: Shivpuri, Madhya Pradesh (PIN: 473551)
- **Language**: Hindi (primary), English (secondary)
- **Tech Literacy**: Low to medium smartphone usage
- **Demographics**: Common citizens, all age groups
- **Access**: Basic smartphones, intermittent internet

## Phase 1: Awareness & Discovery

### How Users Hear About the Platform

#### 1. Offline Channels

**A. Community Outreach**
- **Location**: Public places (markets, bus stands, community centers)
- **Method**: Information stalls with volunteers
- **Materials**: Simple posters, flyers in Hindi

**B. Local Partnerships**
- **Partners**: Local NGOs, community leaders, self-help groups
- **Method**: Word-of-mouth, community meetings
- **Trust Factor**: Leverage existing community trust

**C. Public Announcements**
- **Location**: Panchayat offices, government offices
- **Method**: Notice boards, announcements
- **Message**: Neutral, informational

**D. Radio Announcements**
- **Channels**: Local radio stations
- **Format**: Simple Hindi announcements
- **Frequency**: Daily during pilot phase
- **Script**: See Radio Announcement Script below

#### 2. Online Channels

**A. WhatsApp Groups**
- **Method**: Share in local WhatsApp groups
- **Content**: Simple video demo, voice message
- **Language**: Hindi

**B. Social Media**
- **Platforms**: Facebook (local groups), Instagram
- **Content**: Simple graphics, voice messages
- **Language**: Hindi with English subtitles

**C. SMS Campaign**
- **Target**: Registered mobile numbers in district
- **Message**: Simple Hindi SMS with platform info
- **Frequency**: One-time during pilot launch

   - Explain process clearly
   - Realistic timelines

   - Clear offline indicators

   - Drop-off points

## Summary

This onboarding strategy provides:

1. **Multi-Channel Awareness**: Offline + online channels
2. **Voice-First Approach**: Hindi voice scripts for all interactions
3. **Trust Building**: Clear privacy and transparency messages
4. **Realistic Expectations**: No promises, clear timelines
5. **Feedback Collection**: Regular feedback with action
6. **Do's and Don'ts**: Clear guidelines for pilot phase

The strategy is designed for common citizens with low to medium smartphone literacy, prioritizing voice interaction, trust-building, and clear communication in Hindi.
